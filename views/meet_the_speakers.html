Runik Mehrotra

Runik Mehrotra is the President and CTO of AI labs, Inc, a stealth Artificial Intelligence startup with solutions centered around Financial Services. AI Labs develops small AI solutions to massively revolutionize different industries, automating new areas and maximizing corporate efficiency. In his respective position, he is responsible for developing new business strategies and the development of specific AI products. Previous to AI Labs, Inc, Runik founded Centrix/NYX Group, a global technologies analysis firm specializing in Marketing. Centrix consults with businesses for software development and marketing strategies. Prior to Runik's ventures, he won awards in Mathematics, Software Development, and AI research. Runik is an expert in the fields of Technology Financial Analysis, Artificial Intelligence Solutions, Software Development (particularly Mobile App Development), and Automating Technology.
