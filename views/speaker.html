Become a speaker

Because more people have shown interest in speaking at KUBE than we can accommodate, we have decided to implement an application process. There are multiple steps to this process.

  • Fill out this Google form to get started. Applications are due on January 8.
  • You will receive your initial decision on January 11, which determines whether you advance to the audition round.
  • Auditions will be held on January 16 (the half-day) after school. From these, seven speakers will be selected.
  • The event will take place February 6 from 1:30 to 2:35. Before then, there will be at least one dress rehearsal (date and time TBD).

Please provide an email that you check often, as we will be using this to notify you of selection.

Keep in mind: Talks should be appropriate for a school audience.
