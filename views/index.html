About

KUBE stands for Knowledge, Understanding, Belief and Experience. We believe that International Academy students should have a platform on which they can speak openly about their passions.

What is KUBE?

KUBE is a conference put on by students, for students. It consists of seven 7-minute talks about various topics. The conference was co-founded by two IA seniors in 2014 and the first conference was held in early 2015.

Where/when will the event take place?

The event will be an all-school assembly, held on Thursday, February 4, 2016 from 1:35 - 2:35 PM at the International Academy (Okma Campus).

Where can I find pictures and videos of previous KUBE events?

You can find photos of the 2015 event on our Facebook page and videos on our YouTube channel.
