About

KUBE stands for Knowledge, Understanding, Belief and Experience. We believe that International Academy students should have a platform to speak openly about things they are passionate about.

What is KUBE?

KUBE is a conference put on by students, for students. It consists of seven 7-minute talks about various topics.

Where/when will the event be held?

The event will be a passport assembly, held on Friday, February 6, 2015 from 1:30 - 2:35.
